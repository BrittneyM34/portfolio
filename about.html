About Me

Hi there! I'm Brittney McMahon and I am glad to have you hear to explore my web development portfolio. I graduated from Ashland University with a major in Spanish education and a minor in Instructional Technology. After a year of teaching in a high school, I realized teaching was not my passion and I decided to make a career change. After doing some research I decided to enter the world of tech and study Web Development.

I enjoy web development because I love the idea of takin an idea and seeing it turn into a fully functioning website. I am able to be creative while also thinking logically. I love that there is always something new to learn in every aspect of making an application.

Even though I have a degree in Spanish education, it gave me multiple skills that can be transferred to web development. I developed strong communication skills during my time as a teacher that are valuable assets in the tech community. As well as my minor in Instructional Technology gave me a foundation of understanding of user experience principles, which are crucial for creating user friendly websites
